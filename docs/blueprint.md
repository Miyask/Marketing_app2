# **App Name**: MarketScout Pro

## Core Features:

- Business Profile Extraction: Given a business URL, automatically extract publicly available contact information (e.g., owner name, email, social links, phone number) to quickly build prospect profiles.
- Competitor Identification & Analysis: Input a business's profile or industry keywords and location to identify potential direct and indirect competitors, displaying key data points for strategic analysis.
- Target Client Discovery: Utilize web scraping to find new business leads by specifying industry, geographic location, and other relevant keywords, presenting a list of potential clients.
- AI Marketing Asset Generator: A generative AI tool to create tailored marketing ideas, compelling slogans, and foundational content outlines for assets like logos and brochures, based on the target business's characteristics and industry.
- Lead Management & Storage: Securely store discovered business profiles, contact information, and generated marketing data in a database for ongoing lead tracking and management.
- Campaign Overview & Basic ROI: An intuitive dashboard to track marketing activities associated with managed leads and provide simple tools to estimate potential Return on Investment (ROI) based on user-inputted costs and expected outcomes.
- User Authentication & Profiles: Secure login system with individual user accounts, allowing personalized access to tracked leads and customized marketing insights.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary color, reflecting professionalism and strategic growth, is a sophisticated blue-green (#2273CC). Its hue grounds the application in reliability, while its vibrancy hints at dynamism.
- The background color is a light, almost-white shade of the primary blue-green (#EEF4F8), providing a clean and understated canvas that highlights content and maintains visual harmony.
- An accent color, chosen as a vibrant cyan (#38DBDB), adds a touch of innovation and contrast. It is significantly brighter and more saturated than the primary color, making it ideal for call-to-action elements and important indicators.
- Headlines and prominent text use 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), lending a modern, tech-savvy edge to titles. Body text and longer descriptions use 'Inter' (sans-serif), ensuring excellent readability and a clean, objective feel for detailed information.
- Use crisp, modern, and universally recognizable line icons that clearly communicate their function within a business intelligence context. Icons should be minimal and focused.
- Employ a clean, dashboard-centric layout with a focus on data visualization and clear hierarchies. Information should be easily scannable, with ample whitespace to reduce cognitive load and enhance professionalism.
- Implement subtle, swift animations for transitions between sections, data updates, and user interactions. These should feel efficient and seamless, reinforcing a responsive and sophisticated user experience without being distracting.
